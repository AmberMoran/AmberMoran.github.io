Amber Moran

Amber Moran is employed at Cancer Treatment Centers of America (CTCA) located in Goodyear, AZ. She is the Senior Data Analyst for the Precision Medicine department which is part of the Medicine & Science branch at CTCA. Amber started as a scheduler in 2006 at the CTCA Chicago location and was chosen to configure the scheduling application during the conversion to an enterprise-wide electronic health record. In 2008, she moved with her family to Arizona and helped open the CTCA Phoenix location. It was there that she learned complex hospital operations and went on to lead the scheduling and travel department for nearly five years. After completing her Master's Degree in Healthcare Administration from Grand Canyon University, Amber transferred to the Information Services department as a Clinical Analyst supporting the EHR. Amber managed the clinical reports development and implementation processes as well as implemented Orders Reconciliation Manager in the EHR and a new clinical trials management system called CTMSi. She was recruited to Medicine & Science in the Precision Medicine department three years ago and has supported genetics and genomics initiatives across the enterprise. She has partnered with Medical Oncologists to prepare abstracts for conferences such as ASCO, ESMO, and SGO. Amber lives in Buckeye, AZ, with her husband and four children.
